# J2 Labs

**J2 Labs Holdings LLC (dba eDossea)** — small-team SaaS in dental / health IT.

## Product lines

- **eDossea** — HIPAA-compliant dental patient record and imaging sharing. Flagship. `eDossea.com`
- **AI RCM** — agentic revenue cycle management for dental practices. Early stage.
